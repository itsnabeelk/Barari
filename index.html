About Us

Founded in 1958 by the visionary entrepreneur RATIB MAHMOUD AL ASMAR in Kuwait, the BARARI Group initially excelled in GENERAL trading before diversifying into various sectors such as dates, tea, automobile spare parts, and quality food grains. With a global expansion strategy, the group relocated its headquarters to Dubai in 1993, establishing itself as the cornerstone of theBARARI GROUP. Initially focusing on food sufs, the group underwent a significant transformation in 2011 when it entered the gold business under the dynamic leadership of MR KHALDOUN RATIB AL ASMAR . Leveraging a robust global network, the Barari Group strategically sourced top-quality gold from regions like Africa and Turkey, while also engaging in jewelry trading with imported items from Indonesia, Turkey, Italy, India, and other countries. This strategic shift solidified the group's position as a leading importer and exporter in the market. Expanding further into scrap gold and bullion trading, the group set elevated industry standards, ensuring access to premium-quality gold for its discerning client. The establishment of a cutting-edge gold testing laboratory further exemplified the group's commitment to excellence and integrity, driving its continued growth and success. Today, the BARARI GROUP stands as an industry leader, characterized by visionary leadership, steadfast dedication, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.

GOLDEN

LEGACY

Barari Gold indeed boasts a remarkable legacy in the gold industry. Their expertise and commitment to excellence have solidified their reputation as veterans in the field. The quality and craftsmanship of their gold bars are often celebrated, reflecting their dedication to maintaining the highest standards. Their legacy not only encompasses their products but also their contributions to the industry as a whole, setting benchmarks for integrity and innovation. In the gold industry, the BARARI GROUP distinguishes itself with a wide array of offerings, encompassing both bullion and wholesale jewelry. Their bullion collection features an assortment of gold bars, ranging from TT bars to ounce, mini, and kilo bars, meeting the varied needs of investors. Meanwhile, in the wholesale jewellery sector, they provide an extensive selection of designs crafted in 18k, 21k, and 22k gold, ensuring clients have access to top-quality pieces tailored to their tastes and preferences.
